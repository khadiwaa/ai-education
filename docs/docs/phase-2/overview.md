# Phase 2 — Deep Dives

*Self-paced · Take in any order · Build on Phase 1 foundations*

---

Phase 2 modules go deeper on specific topics. Take them in any order based on what's most relevant to your current work. Each module builds on the Phase 1 foundations.

---

## Technical Track

These modules go deeper on the technical mechanics of AI systems.

| Module | Best for |
|--------|---------|
| [T1: Transformer Architecture](./t1-transformer-architecture) | Engineers who want to understand *why* models behave the way they do |
| [T2: Advanced Prompting & Eval](./t2-advanced-prompting) | Engineers building or improving AI features |
| [T3: Embeddings, RAG & Retrieval](./t3-embeddings-rag) | Engineers working on search, docs AI, or data-grounded features |
| [T4: Agents & Multi-Agent Systems](./t4-agents-multi-agent) | Engineers building agentic workflows or tools |
| [T5: Fine-Tuning & Model Customization](./t5-fine-tuning) | Engineers evaluating when and how to customize models |

---

## Strategic Track

These modules focus on decisions, tradeoffs, and organizational impact.

| Module | Best for |
|--------|---------|
| [S1: Platform Strategy & Vendor Evaluation](./s1-platform-strategy) | Evaluating and choosing AI providers and infrastructure |
| [S2: Cost Modeling & Optimization](./s2-cost-modeling) | Managing AI infrastructure costs at scale |
| [S3: AI Product Strategy](./s3-ai-product-strategy) | Making product decisions about AI features |
| [S4: Team Education & Org Design](./s4-team-education) | Designing learning programs and org structures for AI |
| [S5: Staying Current](./s5-staying-current) | Keeping up with a field that changes monthly |
