# Appendix — Under the Hood

*Optional · Technical · Take in any order*

Everything in Phases 1–3 stands on its own — you never *need* this appendix. It exists
for readers who finish the curriculum and want to know how the machinery actually
works, and for the more technical people on a team adopting this course.

:::info Fair warning
These modules assume comfort with technical material — some involve code, math
notation, and engineering trade-offs. That's the point of them. If that's not your
interest, [Phase 3](/docs/phase-3/overview) is the real capstone of this curriculum.
:::
